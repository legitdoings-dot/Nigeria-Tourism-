Nigerian Cuisine

Signature dishes

  • Jollof Rice

    • Rice cooked with tomatoes, pepper, onions, and spices. Often served at parties and celebrations.

  • Pounded Yam with Egusi

    • Soft pounded yam served with thick melon-seed soup.

  • Suya

    • Spicy grilled meat sold by street vendors.

  • Fried Plantain

    • Sweet fried ripe plantains.

  • Pepper Soup

    • Hot, spicy broth with fish, goat meat, or chicken.
