Northern Nigeria

  1. Yankari National Park

    2. Discover the untamed beauty of Yankari, Nigeria's largest wildlife reserve. Trek through lush savanna dotted with diverse fauna, including elephants, baboons, and various antelopes. Rejuvenate your senses in the Wikki Warm Springs, a natural wonder offering a tranquil escape amidst the wilderness.

  2. Kano Ancient City Walls

    3. Step back in time as you explore the formidable ancient city walls of Kano, a testament to centuries of history and architectural prowess. These historic ramparts once protected a thriving center of trade and culture, and walking along them offers a glimpse into the city's grand past.

  3. Zaria

    4. Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Zaria, a city steeped in history and tradition. Explore its ancient architecture, bustling markets, and experience the unique cultural tapestry woven over generations.

  4. Kaduna

    5. Experience Kaduna as a vibrant cultural hub, where history meets modernity. This city offers a dynamic blend of traditional influences and contemporary life, showcasing Nigeria's diverse heritage.
